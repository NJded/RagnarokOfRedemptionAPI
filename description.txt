Ragnarok of Redemption addon to Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok.

The official DLC for Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok, adding compatibility with Mod of Redemption.

Adds compatibility for mods such as:
Mod of Redemption
Calamity
Thorium

Adds minor compatibility for mods such as:
Secrets of the Shadows
Homeward Ragnarok

This mod does NOT support multiplayer, but this will be fixed with future Mod of Redemption updates.
